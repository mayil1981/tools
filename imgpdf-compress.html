Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a limit to how many files I can compress?
No, you can compress as many files as you need! Our free service has no limits on usage. For very large batches, consider our premium API for automated processing.
Will my compressed files look different?
With our smart compression technology, most files maintain excellent quality even at high compression levels. You can preview the results before downloading.
What happens to my original files?
Your original files are never stored permanently. They are automatically deleted from our servers within 1 hour of processing.
Can I compress multiple files at once?
Currently, our web interface processes one file at a time. For batch processing, check out our desktop application or API services.
